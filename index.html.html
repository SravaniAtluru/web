S.S FARMS

History

S.S farms owned by ATLURI.JAYADEV since 2017 at Atkuru village near Gannavaram. Started with 2000 birds and extended to 6000 birds. Poultry farm is build in his own land and used mineral water for drinking purpose of birds by installing mineral plant. Poultry farm maintaining contains of up and down

Types of birds

There are four types of birds. Parent(big broiler), Small broiler, layer and Country. In S.S FARMS small broiler is available. We should not have different kinds of birds in one farm or at one place.

Work at farm

For every 6000 birds need a pair of people to take care of feed and checking the drinking supply. Need to provide a shelter for workers in order to take care and also for security purposes. Doctor should visit the farm twice a month based on the vaccination schedule.
